Girltalk

What they asked for

For this school project I was assigned to find digital problems for a company that I could solve for. I found the NGO Girltalk where I identified some problems in their representation and how they communicated to the youth.

My role

As a UX Designer I researched about who Girltalk is and who their target audience is. I had to have a bigger understanding of how the youth communicates and what styles they like I also studied how other similar organisations did and what was trending at the moment. As a Web Developer I started building the website with HTML and CSS. I used the framework Astro, to create component to make sure that style was consistent.

As a Project Manager I made sure to organise tasks that needed to be done to be able to comply with deadlines.

Solution

The website ended up with a new online identity, as their current websolution did not speak eye to eye with the target audience.

Customise Me

What they asked for

For this school project I was assigned with a company named CustomiseMe, who needed a better solution for their page where you can build a customised soap. The requirements were to make a cool and user friendly 'create soap' page, as well as create dynamic productlist and productview pages.

My role

As a UX Designer I did a sender research about the company by doing a BMC to find out who they are and what values they have. Afterwards I did a benchmark research to find out how other companies solve this step-by-step purchase, and lastly a trend research to see which trends was currently on the market within their niche. Apart from this I also did information architecture to find out which pages should include what information. Lastly I created some wireframes to set up how the information and elements for each pages should be placed.

As a UI Designer I created a design guide which includes:
- The logo
- The typography, which font to use as headings and paragraphs, which sizes and the weight.
- The colors that should be used and where.
- The photography style.

As a Web Developer I coded the website with HTML and CSS. For the product database i used Restdb and used parameters to fetch each product.

Solution

The final website had a softer look than their original page and had a much more visual and user friendly 'create soap' page.
